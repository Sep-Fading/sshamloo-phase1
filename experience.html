Employed Experience at London Nootropics


Front-End Developer & Web Maintenance from Jan 2022 - Oct 2022


During my gap year after compeleting my A-Level Qualifications, I was working for a Start up E-Commerce company called London Nootropics where I took the role of developing the front-end and maintaining their website with minor involvement in the back-end systems. Here I improved upon my already advanced knowledge of Web Technologies such as HTML and CSS, while learning how to use JavaScript to provide interactivity for the website. Though, a lot of the website has been developed via templates that WordPress provides, I still used my skills to customise the functionality, theme, and the style of the website. For example one of my major additions was their subscription page where users are presented with subscription options that are visually pleasing and match their brand colors. I also custom coded a mega menu to their nav bar which can be seen by clicking "View for Yourself" below.

Personal Project with .NET


Development of a Discord bot using Discord.NET and C# - Summer 2021


I was curious to know how the Bots that carry out useful functions for server management on a Discord server worked. I decided to figure out and stepped upon C# as it seemed that it supported most up-to-date API for creating these bots in the .NET environment. After some research I figured out how to use the API and developed my own Bot that carried a simple purpose; it joined a call and played the audio from any Youtube link it was given. It was pretty useful, now I could listen music with my friends in a call with good quality!
